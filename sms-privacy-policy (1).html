SMS Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026
Operator: Merritt Paul Ltd dba Affordable Self Storage

1. Program Description

Affordable Self Storage offers an SMS messaging program to keep customers informed about storage accounts, payment reminders, facility updates, and special promotions. By opting in, you consent to receive automated text messages at the mobile number you provide.

2. Message Types & Frequency

You may receive: Account Alerts, Payment Reminders, Promotional Offers, and Facility Updates. Message frequency varies — up to 8 messages per month based on account activity and campaigns.

3. Cost and Carriers

Message and data rates may apply. Affordable Self Storage does not charge for SMS; your carrier's standard rates apply. Supported carriers include AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and others. Not all carriers are supported.

4. How to Opt-In

Complete the SMS opt-in form on this website, provide your valid mobile number, check the consent checkbox, and confirm you are authorized to use the number provided. Consent is not required to purchase any goods or services.

5. How to Opt-Out

Opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message, calling 607-770-9342, or visiting 2248 Airport Rd, Johnson City, NY. One final confirmation message will be sent after opt-out is processed.

6. Help and Support

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, call 607-770-9342, or contact us through our website. We're here to help with any questions about the SMS program.

7. Data Collection and Use

We collect your name, mobile number, and account details; message delivery and interaction data; and carrier/device info. This data is used to deliver messages, improve our program, maintain 10DLC compliance, and prevent fraud.

8. Data Retention

Active subscribers: data retained for the duration of your relationship. Opt-out requests: number and opt-out status retained indefinitely. Message logs: retained up to 2 years for compliance purposes.

9. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your mobile number. Information may be shared with SMS service providers (e.g., Twilio, Bandwidth), mobile carriers for delivery, legal authorities when required by law, or in business transfers. All providers are contractually bound to protect your data.

10. Data Security

We implement reasonable physical, technical, and administrative safeguards to protect your information. No electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security but strive to protect your data.

11. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, and port your data, and to opt out of SMS messages at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us using the information in Section 13.

12. 10DLC Compliance

Affordable Self Storage complies with 10DLC regulations established by mobile carriers and The Campaign Registry (TCR). Our program is registered and verified for reliable delivery. All messages are sent from dedicated registered business numbers.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in practices, technology, or legal requirements. Active subscribers will be notified via text of material changes. Continued participation constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

15. Children's Privacy

Our SMS program is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover information was inadvertently collected from a minor, we will promptly delete it.

16. New York Residents

As a New York-based business, we are committed to protecting your privacy rights under New York law. Contact us using Section 13 information for any privacy rights questions.
