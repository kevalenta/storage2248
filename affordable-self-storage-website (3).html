SMS Privacy Policy

Our commitment to protecting your privacy and data

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

SMS Program Operator: Affordable Self Storage (Merritt Paul Ltd dba Affordable Self Storage)

1. Program Description

Affordable Self Storage offers an SMS messaging program to keep customers informed about their storage accounts, payment reminders, facility updates, and special promotions. By opting into this program, you consent to receive automated text messages at the mobile phone number you provide.

2. Message Types and Frequency

You may receive the following types of messages:

  • Account Alerts: Notifications about your storage unit or account status
  • Payment Reminders: Upcoming payment due dates and billing information
  • Promotional Offers: Special discounts, referral bonuses, and limited-time deals
  • Facility Updates: Important announcements about facility operations or access

Message Frequency: You may receive up to 8 messages per month. Message frequency varies based on your account activity and promotional campaigns.

3. Cost and Carriers

Message and data rates may apply. Standard text messaging rates from your mobile carrier will apply to all SMS messages sent and received as part of this program. Affordable Self Storage does not charge for SMS messages, but your carrier's standard rates apply. Please contact your mobile carrier for information about your messaging plan and associated costs.

Supported Carriers: This program is supported by major U.S. carriers including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and others. Not all carriers are supported; please check with your carrier.

4. How to Opt-In

To opt into our SMS program, you must:

  • Complete the SMS opt-in form on this website or at our facility
  • Provide your valid mobile phone number
  • Affirmatively consent by checking the consent checkbox
  • Confirm you are authorized to use the mobile number provided

Your consent is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services from Affordable Self Storage.

5. How to Opt-Out

You can opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by:

  • Text STOP: Reply STOP to any message you receive from us
  • Contact Us: Call us at 607-770-9342 or email us to request removal
  • Visit Our Office: Stop by our facility at 2248 Airport Rd, Johnson City, NY 13790

After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message confirming your removal from the program. You may continue to receive messages for a short period while your opt-out request is processed.

6. Help and Support

For assistance with our SMS program, you can:

  • Text HELP: Reply HELP to any message for assistance
  • Call Us: 607-770-9342 during business hours
  • Email Us: Contact us through our website contact form

7. Data Collection and Use

When you opt into our SMS program, we collect and use the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, mobile phone number, and account details
  • Usage Information: Message delivery status, opt-in/opt-out actions, and interaction with messages
  • Device Information: Mobile carrier information and device type (when provided by carrier)

Purpose of Use: We use this information to:

  • Deliver SMS messages about your account and our services
  • Improve our messaging program and customer experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements, including 10DLC compliance
  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized use

8. Data Retention

We retain your SMS program data as follows:

  • Active Subscribers: Data retained while you remain opted in and for the duration of your customer relationship
  • Opt-Out Requests: Your phone number and opt-out status retained indefinitely to honor your request
  • Message Logs: Delivery records retained for up to 2 years for compliance and customer service purposes

9. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your mobile phone number or personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • SMS Service Providers: Third-party platforms that facilitate message delivery (e.g., Twilio, Bandwidth)
  • Mobile Carriers: Your wireless carrier as necessary to deliver messages
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law, court order, or legal process
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

All third-party service providers are required to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

10. Data Security

We implement reasonable physical, technical, and administrative safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request information about the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from SMS messages at any time
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information provided in Section 13 below.

12. 10DLC Compliance

Affordable Self Storage complies with 10DLC (10-Digit Long Code) regulations established by mobile carriers and The Campaign Registry (TCR). Our SMS program is registered and verified to ensure reliable message delivery and prevent spam. All messages are sent from dedicated business phone numbers registered for commercial messaging use.
